Introduction to 21 Savage

21 Savage - Who has multiple hits including fan favorite "Glock in My Lap"

Shéyaa Bin Abraham-Joseph, known professionally as 21 Savage, is a British rapper, songwriter, and record producer based in Atlanta, Georgia. Born on October 22, 1992, in Plaistow, Newham, London, 21 Savage rose to prominence with his unique style and gritty lyrics. 21 Savage gained widespread recognition with his debut mixtape "Savage Mode" in collaboration with producer Metro Boomin, which spawned the hit single "X" featuring Future. His distinct delivery and tales of street life quickly set him apart in the rap scene. The rapper's discography includes successful albums like "Issa Album" and "I Am > I Was," each showcasing his growth as an artist. One of his standout tracks, "Glock in My Lap," has become a fan favorite, solidifying his position in the rap game. Beyond his music, 21 Savage is known for his advocacy work and community engagement. He has been active in addressing social issues and promoting financial literacy, demonstrating a commitment to making a positive impact. With his unique voice, compelling storytelling, and unapologetic approach to his craft, 21 Savage continues to leave an indelible mark on the world of rap music.

Shéyaa Bin Abraham-Joseph, better known as 21 Savage, achieved widespread acclaim with his iconic track "Glock in My Lap." Released as part of his album "I Am > I Was," the song captivates listeners with its menacing beat and raw, introspective lyrics. The track delves into 21 Savage's experiences navigating the challenges of street life. "Glock in My Lap" showcases 21 Savage's signature style, blending authenticity and vulnerability. The haunting production, coupled with his distinct delivery, makes it a standout in contemporary rap. The accompanying music video further intensifies the impact, providing a visual narrative that complements the song's powerful storytelling.
